Features

Everything you need to master your money

From AI-powered scanning to deep financial insights — SnapSpend puts you in complete control of your spending.

AI Receipt Scanning

Snap a photo and GPT-4o extracts merchant, total, date, and line items automatically — with 99% accuracy.

Smart Categories

Receipts sorted into 12+ categories automatically. Create custom categories with custom icons and colors.

Smart Budgets

Set monthly budgets and per-category targets. Track progress in real time with visual indicators.
